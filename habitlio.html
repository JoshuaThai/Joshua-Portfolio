Habit.lio is my capstone project for my Computer Science BS.

In this project, I worked in a team of 6 to develop a full-stack habit tracking chrome extension that contains all of the core features of a habit tracking application such ass:

  • Creating, editing, and deleting habits
  • Tracking habit completion
  • Viewing habit history and statistics
  • Customizing habit reminders
  • Streaks
  • Social networking features (Friends, Affirmations, and Messaging)

I leverage technologies such as React.js, Firebase, and Node.js to build a robust and scalable Chrome Extension.
