Habit.lio



Try on GitHub

Habit.lio is my capstone project for my Computer Science BS. In this project, I worked in a team of 6 to develop a habit tracking chrome extension that contains all of the core features of a habit tracking application with a social networking component:

  • Creating, editing, and deleting habits
  • Tracking habit completion
  • Viewing habit history and statistics
  • Customizing habit reminders
  • Streaks
  • Social networking features (Friends, Affirmations, and Messaging)
