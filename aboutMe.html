About Me

Hello, I am Joshua Thaisananikone, a software engineer and game developer based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. From a young age, I have been fascinated by technology, whether it was playing video games or exploring software. After witnessing the piloting of a suicide-prevention training video game, I knew tech was my calling. I wanted to touch the lives of others, and technology gave me the platform to do so. My passion for software engineering stems from a deep love for problem-solving, guided by the belief that even the most complex problems can be solved through perseverance and continuous learning.

I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. As an undergraduate, I was awarded the Anu and Satya Nadella Scholarship, a prestigious full-ride award funded by Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella. This scholarship is selectively awarded to 15 incoming freshmen pursuing degrees in Computer Science, Data Science, or Information Technology.

Recently, I co-founded Dead Signal Studios with a friend. We are passionate about creating engaging, story-driven horror games that deliver immersive experiences and create a meaningful social impact. To reach a diverse audience, we plan to produce both standalone indie titles and Roblox games. Our debut title is Patient_Zero.exe, which we developed for the gamedev.js 2026 game jam.

I am currently seeking opportunities in software engineering, game development, and quality assurance, and I look forward to building software that changes lives.
