About Me

Hello, I am Joshua, a software engineer and game developer based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. From a very young age, I have always been intrigued by technology whether it be games or software. After witnessing the piloting of a suicide prevention training video game, I knew technology was the place for me! I wanted to touch the lives of others and technology would enable that! My fascination for software engineering comes from my love for problem-solving and games. My belief is that regardless if a problem is complex or not, it can be solved through perseverance and utilizing my past experience. I have created many web applications in class and through my own personal time.

I graduate from the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. As a student, I was awarded the Anu and Satya Nadella Scholarship, a full-ride scholarship funded by Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella. The Anu and Satya Nadella Scholarship is offered to 15 incoming freshmen students majoring in the STEM at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee. The scholarship is exclusively for Computer Science, Data Science and Information Technology majors only.

I recently co-founded a game studio, Dead Signal Studios, with a friend. We are passionate about creating engaging and innovative games that can provide entertainment and value to our players. We plan to bring story-driven horror games that deliver immersive experiences and create social impact. We are planning to produce indie games and Roblox games to engage a wide audience. Currently, our debut title will be Patient_Zero.exe, which is a game that we created for the gamedev.js 2026 game jam.

I am looking for opportunities in software engineering, game development, and quality assurance. I am excited to see where my career will lead me and I am looking forward to building software that can change the lives of others.
